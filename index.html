Frequently Asked Questions

Our AI uses different teaching approaches based on age. For children, it employs colorful visuals, simple language, games, and rewards to maintain engagement. For adults, it focuses on practical scenarios, complex grammar, and professional vocabulary. The system continuously learns from interactions to better personalize the experience.

Our speech recognition technology is highly accurate and can understand various accents and speech patterns. It's specifically trained to recognize common pronunciation challenges for language learners. The system provides detailed feedback on pronunciation, intonation, and rhythm, helping you improve your speaking skills effectively.

Yes, parents have access to a dedicated dashboard that shows their child's learning progress, time spent, skills improved, and areas that need more practice. Parents can also review conversation histories, completed activities, and receive weekly progress reports via email. The platform includes parental controls to manage session duration and content.

For optimal results, we recommend 15-20 minutes daily for children and 20-30 minutes for adults. Consistency is more important than duration. Our AI will suggest ideal session lengths based on age, attention span, and learning goals. The platform also includes features to prevent fatigue and maintain engagement during longer sessions.

While most features require an internet connection for the AI to function optimally, we offer offline mode for certain activities like vocabulary review, reading practice, and pre-downloaded lessons. Once back online, progress syncs automatically, and the AI adapts future lessons based on offline activity performance.
